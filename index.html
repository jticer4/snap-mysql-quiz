CHAR sets a fixed size for your data, whereas VARCHAR holds a variable amount, so if you use less characters than you expected it won't be wasting space; However, CHAR is must faster than VARCHAR

The limit can be modified to suit your needs, however CHAR can be 255 characters at its max
